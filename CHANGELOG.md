Initial public alpha.

- Processed-data CSV export, image copy/download, and native sharing where available.
- Public client entry and server-safe static entry.
- Optional editorial typography stylesheet with bundled Fraunces and JetBrains Mono.
- Packed-consumer smoke test and publish lifecycle checks.
